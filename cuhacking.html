  • Hardware hack with Gabriel Elkadiki, Tony Kim and Tauheed Elahee.
  • WeatherMe-LTE is a mobile weather station with internet/LTE connectivity.
  • Uses an Arduino Uno and Particle Boron to collect data from a barometer, thermometer and hygrometer.
  • Outputs the data to a Bootstrap 4 webpage and graphs the data over time using Canvas.js.
