CanDev 2020

January 18th-19th 2020 - Ottawa, ON

Previous Next
  • 24 hackathon put on by departments of the Canadian federal goverment.
  • Candidates were assigned obtuse business problems and solutions were presented to department executives at the end of the event.
  • Business case was "Building an Intelligent Curriculum Framework."
  • Team consisted of Wilfred Dondji, Jeff Cole and myself.
  • We were challenged to identify gaps, related and/or conflicting content, and outdated content from the Canadian School of Public Service's learning curriculum, and develop a robust curriculum architecture framework.
  • Final backend was produced by Wilfred Dondji and used a Python server to implement machine learning to associate courses with certain skill sets and identify redundancies.
  • I built a front-end web app and populated a dynamic wordcloud using an R script made with wordcloud2. The words were mined from the course listings using tidytext.
